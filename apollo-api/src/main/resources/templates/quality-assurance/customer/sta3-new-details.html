STA 3: QUESTIONNAIRE FOR INITIAL FACTORY ASSESSMENT

1. This document shall be completed by the applicant and shall be returned together with the application form STA/1 and/or covering letter.

2. Supplements may be included where it is necessary to expand any statements.

3. A separate document shall be completed for each factory involved.

4. The statements shall relate to the facilities available as of the date of completion of the form.

5. The information given in this document shall be treated in Confidence.
